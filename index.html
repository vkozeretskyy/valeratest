Our Fruits and Vegetables Shop

Akiki freres; Fruits and Vegetables Shop, is situated on Antelias main road. It was established in 1985 by Chaina Akiki.
Back then it was your simple neighborhood fruits and vegetables stand. Twenty-four years later, Chaina’s sons,Rany, Ralph and Tony have taken this concept to a whole new level, they followed their instinct, they are now the "Fruits and Vegetables Shop" bringing produce from anywhere in the world.

In 2009 the brothers moved to a two story fruits and vegetables center.
They have maintained the freshness and variety their father was always sure to offer, but have also upgraded this concept to include nature's produce that add ease, flavor and freshness to all their customers.

Akiki Freres offers you all the fruits and vegetables you desire all year round. What isn’t in season locally is imported in order to satisfy all cravings.
